Detailed Description

template<class Config>
struct honey::lockfree::Mem< Config >::ThreadData::Recycle

Lock-free list of recycled free nodes.

One bin is maintained for each other thread, so at most there are two threads accessing a bin (this + other). The producer thread (other) adds to the tail and the consumer thread (this) removes from the head. In this way, contention for the recycle bin is completely eliminated.
